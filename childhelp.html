Safety 550

Guide

Hello! This Google Chrome extension is designed to help you gain a better idea of how to safely browse the Internet by giving you information about the websites you visit. This information comes from website reviews provided by adult users from all around; the adult(s) that installed this for you may even add some reviews themselves!

Based on the reviews for each website, this extension can go into detail about what makes the website you are going on safe or unsafe. When adults agree that a website is unsafe, this extension will warn you when you try to go to it and show the reason(s) why.

To be clear, this is not the same thing as a parental control app. You will never be blocked from visiting a website, even if adults consider it dangerous. Also, your browser history will not be shared with the adult(s) that installed this. The purpose of this extension is only for you to learn good browsing habits.
